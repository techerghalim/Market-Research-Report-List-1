<p><p>Ice therapy machines are categorized into two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic machines provide a consistent flow of cold water or ice, often with programmable settings for temperature and duration, ensuring efficient treatment without user intervention. In contrast, manual machines require users to control the ice application and adjust settings manually, offering flexibility but demanding more user input. Both types cater to pain management and recovery needs, making them essential tools in sports medicine and rehabilitation practices.</p></p>
