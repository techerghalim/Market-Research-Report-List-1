<p><p>Spinning Flow Formed Wheels are categorized into two primary market types: Casting Spinning and Forging Spinning. </p><p>Casting Spinning involves shaping molten metal into wheel forms, allowing for complex designs and shapes with excellent surface finish. This method is typically cost-effective for mass production. </p><p>In contrast, Forging Spinning uses mechanical force to shape solid materials, resulting in higher strength and durability. This method is ideal for high-performance applications, where weight reduction and structural integrity are critical, though it tends to have higher production costs.</p></p>
