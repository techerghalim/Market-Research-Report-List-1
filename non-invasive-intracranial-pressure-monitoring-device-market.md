<p><strong>The Non-invasive Intracranial Pressure Monitoring Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transcranial Doppler Ultrasonography</li><li>Tympanic Membrane Displacement (TMD)</li><li>Optic Nerve Sheath Diameter</li><li>MRI/CT</li><li>Fundoscopy (Papilledema)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-invasive intracranial pressure monitoring device market includes various methods for assessing intracranial pressure without invasive techniques. Transcranial Doppler Ultrasonography evaluates blood flow velocity in cerebral arteries, providing insights into pressure changes. Tympanic Membrane Displacement (TMD) measures tympanic membrane displacement related to ICP fluctuations. The Optic Nerve Sheath Diameter assesses swelling of the optic nerve sheath via ultrasound. MRI/CT imaging offers advanced visualization of brain structures and pressure effects, while Fundoscopy evaluates papilledema indicative of increased ICP through retinal examination.</p></p>
