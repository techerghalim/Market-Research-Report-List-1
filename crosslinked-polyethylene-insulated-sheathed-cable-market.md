<p><p>The Crosslinked Polyethylene Insulated Sheathed Cable market is divided into two main types: single core and multi-core cables. Single core crosslinked polyethylene insulated cables consist of one conductor, providing flexibility and ease of installation in various applications. In contrast, multi-core crosslinked polyethylene insulated cables contain multiple conductors within a single sheath, allowing for complex wiring solutions in industrial and residential settings. Both types offer excellent electrical insulation, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for diverse electrical applications.</p></p>
