<p><ul><li>Standard-Density Integrated Arrays</li><li>High-Density Integrated Arrays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED array market is categorized into Standard-Density Integrated Arrays and High-Density Integrated Arrays. Standard-Density Integrated Arrays feature a moderate arrangement of LEDs, providing a balance between performance and cost, suitable for general lighting applications. In contrast, High-Density Integrated Arrays incorporate tightly packed LEDs, enabling higher brightness and efficiency, making them ideal for specialized uses like high-performance displays and advanced automotive lighting. Both types cater to varying technological needs and application requirements in the LED industry.</p></p>
