<p><p>The industrial robotics market encompasses various types tailored to specific applications. Articulated robots feature rotary joints, offering flexibility for complex tasks. Parallel robots, designed with multiple arms, excel in speed and stability for precise operations. SCARA robots provide high-speed, horizontal movement, ideal for assembly tasks. Cylindrical robots utilize rotary and linear motions for efficient operations in a cylindrical work area. Cartesian robots, or gantry robots, operate along three linear axes, making them suitable for precise pick-and-place applications. Each type contributes uniquely to automation efficiency.</p></p>
