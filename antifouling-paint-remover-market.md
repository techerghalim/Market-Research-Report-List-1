<p><p>The antifouling paint remover market consists of two primary types: direct use and use after water dissolving. Direct use removers are formulated for immediate application on surfaces, effectively breaking down marine growth and paint layers without needing preparation. Both types cater to different user preferences and operational procedures within the marine maintenance and repair sectors.</p></p>
