<p><p>The marine bilge water separators market primarily includes three types: gravity bilge water separators, centrifugal bilge water separators, and others. Gravity separators utilize differences in density to separate oil from water through a settling process. Centrifugal separators employ high-speed rotating mechanisms to enhance separation efficiency by utilizing centrifugal force. Other types may include advanced technologies like membrane filters and electrocoagulation systems. These separators are essential for ensuring compliance with environmental regulations by effectively cleaning bilge water before discharge.</p></p>
