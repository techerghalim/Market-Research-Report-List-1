<p><p>The lasers used in pigmented lesion treatment include Q-Switched Ruby Laser, Q-Switched Nd:YAG Laser, and Q-Switched Alexandrite Laser. The Q-Switched Nd:YAG Laser penetrates deeper layers of skin, making it suitable for a variety of deeper lesions and vascular lesions. The Q-Switched Alexandrite Laser offers versatility with its rapid wavelength and pulse duration, effectively treating both superficial and deeper pigmented lesions.</p></p>
