<p><p>The PCB aviation connector market is categorized into two primary types: circular and rectangular connectors. Circular connectors are typically used in environments requiring compact, robust designs, providing excellent resistance to mechanical stress and environmental factors. They feature a rounded shape that allows for efficient cable management. Both types are critical for ensuring reliable electrical connections in aviation systems.</p></p>
