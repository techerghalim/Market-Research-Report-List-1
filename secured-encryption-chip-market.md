<p><strong>The Secured Encryption Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The secured encryption chip market is categorized into two main types: contact and contactless. Contact chips require physical contact with a reader to transmit data, typically used in smart cards and payment systems. These chips ensure secure transactions by requiring precise alignment with the reader. In contrast, contactless chips utilize radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing data exchange without direct contact. This facilitates fast and convenient transactions, making contactless solutions preferred for mobile payments and access control applications.</p></p>
