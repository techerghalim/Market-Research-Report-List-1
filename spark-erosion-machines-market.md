<p><p>The spark erosion machines market is segmented into three main types: wire cutting machines, die sinking EDM, and hole drilling EDM. Wire cutting machines utilize a thin, electrically charged wire to precisely cut through conductive materials, making them ideal for intricate shapes. Die sinking EDM employs a shaped electrode to create complex cavities in dies and molds. Hole drilling EDM specializes in creating deep, narrow holes in various materials, often used in aerospace and automotive applications, enhancing precision in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
