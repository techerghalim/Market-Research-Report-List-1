<p><p>EVA interlayer for photovoltaic applications is vital for solar module encapsulation, providing protection and enhancing performance. The Normal EVA film is the standard choice, offering durability and adhesion for conventional solar panels. In contrast, Anti-PID EVA film is engineered to combat Potential Induced Degradation (PID), a phenomenon that can reduce solar panel efficiency over time. By using Anti-PID EVA film, manufacturers improve the longevity and reliability of modules, making it a preferred option in high-performance solar installations.</p></p>
