<p><p>The Computational Drug Discovery market encompasses various approaches, including Structure-based Drug Design (SBDD), Ligand-based Drug Design (LBDD), and Sequence-based Approaches. SBDD relies on the 3D structures of biological targets to identify and optimize drug candidates. LBDD focuses on known active compounds to predict and design new molecules with similar properties. Sequence-based Approaches involve analyzing genetic and protein sequences to find potential targets and biomolecules, providing a framework for novel drug design, particularly in the realm of personalized medicine.</p></p>
