<p><p>Chuck controllers operate in two main market types: AC and DC. AC type controllers utilize alternating current to manage the rotation and torque of chucks, providing smooth operation and efficiency in high-speed applications. In contrast, DC type controllers employ direct current, offering precise speed control and quick response times, ideal for applications requiring high torque at low speeds. Both market types cater to various industries, ensuring versatility in performance and applications while meeting specific operational requirements.</p></p>
