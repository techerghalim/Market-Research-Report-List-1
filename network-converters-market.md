<p><p>The network converters market is segmented into three main types: Copper to Fiber, Fiber to Fiber, and Copper to Copper converters. Copper to Fiber converters facilitate the transition from copper cabling to fiber optics, enhancing data transmission speed and distance. Fiber to Fiber converters enable seamless connection between different fiber types or wavelengths, ensuring compatibility in diverse network environments. Copper to Copper converters maintain and extend existing copper networks without the need for more advanced infrastructure, ensuring cost-effective solutions for businesses.</p></p>
