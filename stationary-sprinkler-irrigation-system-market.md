<p><ul><li>Center Pivot</li><li>Lateral Move</li><li>Solid Set</li><li>Other Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary sprinkler irrigation system market comprises several types designed for efficient water distribution. Center pivot systems feature rotating arms that move around a central pivot point, delivering water uniformly across large fields. Lateral move systems employ a linear motion along straight paths, suitable for rectangular fields. Solid set systems consist of fixed sprinkler heads providing consistent coverage. Other types may include combination systems or specialized designs for specific crops. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on field size, crop type, and water availability.</p></p>
