<p><ul><li>Quartz Mask</li><li>Soda Mask</li><li>Relief Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Semiconductor Photomask market comprises three main types: Quartz Masks, Soda Masks, and Relief Plates. Quartz Masks, made from high-purity silicon dioxide, are favored for their optical clarity and durability in photolithography. Soda Masks utilize soda-lime glass, offering cost-effectiveness for less critical applications. Relief Plates provide a three-dimensional surface profile, enhancing resolution and feature definition in specific lithography processes. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to varying requirements in semiconductor manufacturing and advancing technology.</p></p>
