<p><p>The sexually transmitted diseases (STD) drug market comprises three main types: antiviral/antiretrovirals, antibiotics, and vaccines. Antiviral and antiretroviral drugs are used to manage viral infections like HIV and herpes, inhibiting viral replication. Antibiotics treat bacterial STDs, such as chlamydia and gonorrhea, effectively eliminating infection. Vaccines prevent certain STDs, such as HPV and hepatitis B, by enhancing the immune response.
