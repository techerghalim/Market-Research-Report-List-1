<p><p>The construction drone market comprises two main types: fixed-wing drones and rotary-wing drones. Fixed-wing drones are characterized by their longer flight times and greater coverage, making them suitable for large-scale surveying and mapping projects. In contrast, rotary-wing drones, commonly known as quadcopters, excel in maneuverability and can hover in place, making them ideal for site inspections and monitoring. Each type serves distinct functions within the construction industry, enhancing efficiency, safety, and data collection capabilities.</p></p>
