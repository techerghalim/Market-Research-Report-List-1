<p><p>Trypsin EDTA Solution is a commonly used enzymatic preparation in cell culture and biotechnology for the dissociation of adherent cells from the substrate. It contains trypsin, a proteolytic enzyme that breaks down proteins, and EDTA, a chelating agent that helps to bind calcium and magnesium ions, which are essential for cell adhesion.
