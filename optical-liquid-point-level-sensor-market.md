<p><strong>The Optical Liquid Point Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Liquid Point Level Sensor market can be divided into two main types: contact and contactless sensors. Contact sensors interact directly with the liquid, using light transmission properties to detect levels, making them suitable for various liquids, including those with particulate matter. In contrast, contactless sensors operate by emitting light that reflects off the liquid surface, enabling level detection without physical contact. This makes contactless sensors ideal for corrosive or viscous liquids, enhancing safety and longevity in various applications.</p></p>
