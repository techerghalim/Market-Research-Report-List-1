<p><p>The Radio Shuttle System market comprises two primary types: LIFO (Last In, First Out) and FIFO (First In, First Out). In a LIFO system, the most recently added pallets are the first to be retrieved, optimizing storage for goods with a shorter shelf life. Conversely, the FIFO system prioritizes the retrieval of the oldest pallets first, ensuring older inventory is used before newer stock. Both systems enhance warehouse efficiency and inventory management, catering to different operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
