<p><p>The disposable vinyl gloves market is primarily divided into two types: powered vinyl gloves and powder-free vinyl gloves. Powered vinyl gloves are coated with a light layer of powder, usually cornstarch, to facilitate easy donning and removal, ideal for quick tasks in various settings. Conversely, powder-free vinyl gloves are designed without any powdered coating, reducing the risk of allergic reactions and contamination. These gloves are preferred in healthcare, food service, and other sensitive environments where hygiene is paramount.</p></p>
