<p><p>The wood chipper market comprises several types, primarily classified as drum chippers, drum-style chippers, and disc-style chippers. Drum chippers utilize rotating drums to efficiently shred wood into chips, offering high processing capacity. Drum-style chippers feature a similar design but may vary in blade arrangement and feed mechanisms. Disc-style chippers, on the other hand, use a horizontal disc with sharp knives to slice wood, providing precise chip sizes. Other market variations may include mobile or portable chippers designed for specific applications.</p></p>
