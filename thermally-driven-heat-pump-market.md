<p><p>The thermally driven heat pump market consists of various types based on their installation and operational mechanisms. Vertical closed loop systems utilize deep boreholes for efficient heat exchange, making them suitable for limited space. Horizontal closed loop systems are installed underground in trenches, ideal for larger areas. Open loop systems draw water from a well or surface source, using the water directly as a heat transfer medium. Other markets include innovative designs like hybrid systems, integrating multiple technologies for enhanced efficiency.</p></p>
