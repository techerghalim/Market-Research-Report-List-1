<p><p>The polyester straps market comprises primarily two types: Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) straps and Polypropylene (PP) straps. PET straps are widely used for heavy-duty applications due to their strength, durability, and resistance to stretching, making them ideal for securing loads in shipping and logistics. In contrast, PP straps are lighter and more flexible, suitable for lighter packaging and bundling tasks. Both types serve diverse industries, from packaging and construction to logistics, offering efficient solutions for securing products during transportation.</p></p>
