<p><ul><li>Hydronic Heating Systems</li><li>Electric Snowmelt Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snowmelt systems market primarily includes hydronic heating systems and electric snowmelt systems. Hydronic systems use heated liquid circulated through pipes under the surface to melt snow and ice efficiently. These systems are often powered by boilers or heat pumps and are ideal for larger areas. In contrast, electric snowmelt systems utilize heating cables installed beneath the surface, providing a more direct and easily controllable approach. These systems are suitable for smaller spaces and quick installation, catering to diverse snow management needs.</p></p>
