<p><strong>The Marine Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Steel Pipe</li><li>Straight Seam Submerged Arc Welded Steel Pipe (SAW)</li><li>Straight Seam High Frequency Resistance Welded Steel Pipe (HFERW)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine pipes market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Seamless steel pipes are forged from solid billets, offering high strength and resistance to corrosion, ideal for high-pressure environments. Straight seam submerged arc welded steel pipes (SAW) are characterized by their continuous weld, providing robustness for large-diameter applications. Straight seam high frequency resistance welded steel pipes (HFERW) use high-frequency currents to join edges, resulting in a cost-efficient option for structural frameworks and transport of fluids in marine settings.</p></p>
