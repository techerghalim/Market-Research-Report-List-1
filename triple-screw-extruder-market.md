<p><ul><li>Parallel</li><li>Nonparallel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The triple screw extruder market is categorized into two main types: parallel and non-parallel. Parallel triple screw extruders feature three screws that run alongside each other, providing uniform mixing and enhanced output efficiency, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent material properties. In contrast, non-parallel triple screw extruders have screws that are arranged at angles, allowing for better material transport and more complex processing capabilities. Each type serves distinct applications based on production requirements and material characteristics.</p></p>
