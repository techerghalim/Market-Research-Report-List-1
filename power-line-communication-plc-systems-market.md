<p><ul><li>Narrowband PLC</li><li>Broadband PLC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) systems are categorized into Narrowband PLC and Broadband PLC. Narrowband PLC operates at lower frequencies, typically used for utility metering and monitoring applications, offering limited data rates and longer communication ranges. In contrast, Broadband PLC provides higher data rates, enabling multimedia applications like internet access and video streaming. This type uses higher frequency bands and is suitable for home networking and smart grid solutions, facilitating enhanced communication and connectivity capabilities over existing power lines.</p></p>
