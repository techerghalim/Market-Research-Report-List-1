<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) adhesives market encompasses various types, each serving specific applications. Acrylic-based adhesives offer fast curing and excellent thermal resistance. Cyanoacrylate-based adhesives provide rapid bonding and are ideal for smaller components. Epoxy-based adhesives exhibit strong adhesion and durability, suitable for harsher environments. Silicone-based adhesives offer flexibility and electrical insulation, suitable for high-temperature applications. Polyurethane-based adhesives provide robust bonding and moisture resistance, making them versatile for various electronic assembly processes. Each type caters to distinct performance requirements in PCB manufacturing.</p></p>
