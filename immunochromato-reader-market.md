<p><ul><li>Absorption Method</li><li>Fluorescence Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochromato reader market consists of two primary types: the absorption method and the fluorescence method. The absorption method relies on the visual detection of colored lines produced by antibodies binding to target antigens, making it simple and cost-effective for rapid tests. In contrast, the fluorescence method employs fluorescent labels that emit light upon reaction, offering enhanced sensitivity and specificity. These methods are widely utilized in diagnostics, including infectious disease detection and biomarker analysis, driving innovations in point-of-care testing.</p></p>
