<p><p>The stationary sander market comprises two main types: electric and pneumatic sanders. Electric stationary sanders are powered by electricity, offering efficiency and ease of use for various sanding applications in workshops and industries. They typically provide consistent speed and power, making them ideal for large-scale or precision work. Pneumatic stationary sanders, on the other hand, utilize compressed air, which allows for potentially higher operating speeds and lighter weight. These are favored in environments where air power is readily available, offering smooth operation with less user fatigue.</p></p>
