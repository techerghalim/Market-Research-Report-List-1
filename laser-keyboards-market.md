<p><p>The laser keyboard market is primarily segmented into two types: USB and Bluetooth devices. USB laser keyboards connect directly to computers or laptops via a USB port, providing a stable connection and minimal latency. In contrast, Bluetooth laser keyboards offer wireless convenience, allowing users to connect to a variety of devices like tablets and smartphones without the need for physical cables. Both types cater to different user preferences, with USB being preferred for stationary use and Bluetooth for portability and flexibility.</p></p>
