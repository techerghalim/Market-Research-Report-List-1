<p><ul><li>Single Drill Bit</li><li>Double Drill Bit</li><li>Three Drill Bit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PDC drill bits are categorized based on the number of cutting elements they feature. Single drill bits have one set of polycrystalline diamond compact (PDC) cutters, ideal for simpler drilling tasks. Double drill bits incorporate two sets, offering improved cutting efficiency and enhanced durability for more complex applications. Three drill bits combine three sets of PDC cutters, providing superior performance in challenging formations, allowing for faster penetration rates and longer bit life. Each type is tailored to specific drilling requirements.</p></p>
