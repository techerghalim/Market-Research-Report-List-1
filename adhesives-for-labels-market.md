<p><p>The adhesives for labels market primarily features two types: permanent and removable adhesives. Permanent adhesives create a strong bond, ensuring that labels stay firmly attached over time, making them ideal for products requiring long-term identification. In contrast, removable adhesives allow labels to be easily peeled off without leaving residue, making them suitable for temporary applications or trial labels. The choice between these adhesives depends on the desired permanence and the specific requirements of the labeling application.</p></p>
