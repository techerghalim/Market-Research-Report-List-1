<p><p>The **Combustion Catalysts Market** is segmented into **Metallic Catalysts** and **Nonmetallic Catalysts**. Metallic catalysts, often composed of transition metals like platinum or palladium, enhance combustion efficiency by accelerating reactions at lower temperatures, reducing emissions. Nonmetallic catalysts, such as zeolites or metal oxides, offer cost-effective and thermally stable alternatives, ideal for high-temperature applications. Both types optimize fuel combustion, improve energy efficiency, and minimize pollutants, catering to industries like automotive, power generation, and manufacturing, based on specific operational and environmental requirements.</p></p>
