<p><p>The PVC pipe cement market is categorized into three primary types based on viscosity and application: Regular Bodied PVC Pipe Cement, Medium Bodied PVC Pipe Cement, and Heavy Bodied PVC Pipe Cement. Regular Bodied cement is ideal for small diameter pipes and quick bonding, Medium Bodied cement offers increased thickness for larger pipes and more durable applications, while Heavy Bodied cement provides the highest viscosity, ensuring robust adhesion for larger-scale plumbing and construction projects requiring stronger joints.</p></p>
