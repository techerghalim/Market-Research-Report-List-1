Proportional valves allow for variable flow control, ensuring optimal respiratory support by adjusting airflow based on patient needs. Other valves may include manual or electronic alternatives designed for specific applications. Each valve type plays a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of breathing machines in medical settings.</p></p>
