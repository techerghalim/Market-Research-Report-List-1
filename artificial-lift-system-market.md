Progressive cavity pumps (PCP) employ a rotating mechanism for consistent flow, whereas plunger lifts use a plunger to manage fluids in wells. Gas lift systems inject gas to reduce fluid density, aiding extraction. Other emerging technologies also contribute to this diverse market.</p></p>
