<p><p>Exterior Insulation and Finish Systems (EIFS) are cladding systems that provide both insulation and a decorative finish. The market is primarily divided into two types: polymer-based and polymer-modified EIFS. Polymer-based EIFS uses pure polymer resins for flexibility and moisture resistance, offering superior durability and energy efficiency. In contrast, polymer-modified EIFS incorporates additives to enhance adhesion and improve water resistance while providing a more adaptable application process. Both types aim to improve insulation performance and enhance aesthetic appeal in building facades.</p></p>
