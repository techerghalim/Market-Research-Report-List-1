<p><p>The Germanium (Ge) evaporation materials market comprises four primary types: granular, wire, block, and pellet. Granular Germanium consists of small particles suitable for thin film deposition. Wire type is fashioned into wires for controlled evaporation in vacuum systems. Block type features solid blocks, ensuring high purity and consistent evaporation rates, while pellet type consists of compressed discs of Germanium, offering efficient usage in evaporators. These variations cater to diverse industrial applications, including electronics and optoelectronics, enhancing versatility in material deposition.</p></p>
