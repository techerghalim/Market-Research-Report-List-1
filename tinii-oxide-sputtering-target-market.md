Rotatable Transformation targets allow continuous sputtering by rotating the target material, enhancing uniformity and efficiency in film deposition processes. In contrast, Non-Rotating Type targets are static, which may limit the uniformity of the deposited layers but can be more cost-effective for specific applications. Both types serve distinct needs within the semiconductor and electronics industries for producing high-quality thin films.</p></p>
